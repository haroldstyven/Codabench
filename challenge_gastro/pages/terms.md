# Terms and Conditions

---

## Eligibility and registration

- Participation is open to individuals and teams from academic and research institutions worldwide.
- Each team must register with a single valid email address. Multiple accounts for the same team are not permitted and will result in disqualification.
- Teams may include any number of members.

---

## Submission policy

- **Limits:** 5 submissions per day · 100 submissions total per team.
- Submissions that do not comply with the required format will be rejected and will count toward the daily limit.
- Uploading predictions derived from manual inspection or labeling of the evaluation set is strictly prohibited.

---

## Data usage

- The GastroCorp dataset is released under CC BY-NC 4.0. It may be used for research and academic purposes with proper attribution. Commercial use is not permitted.
- The use of external datasets and publicly available pre-trained models is permitted and encouraged.
- The validation set labels are withheld during the challenge and must not be inferred, scraped, or approximated through any means other than model prediction.

---

## Reproducibility

- Participants are expected to use fixed random seeds and document their experimental setup.
- The top-ranked teams at the close of the evaluation phase may be requested to share their code and a brief methodology description to verify their results.

---

## Post-challenge

- After the evaluation phase closes, the gold labels for the development and test sets will be released publicly on the Zenodo repository.
- The organizers reserve the right to present an analysis of the challenge results in an academic publication, with acknowledgment of all participating teams.
