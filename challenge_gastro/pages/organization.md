## Organizers

### Dr. Edwin Puertas
**Research Professor · Universidad Tecnológica de Bolívar**

Artificial Intelligence Software Architect and Natural Language Processing (NLP) Researcher with 20 years of experience. Director of the Ph.D. and Master's Programs in Engineering at Universidad Tecnológica de Bolívar and active member of the Artificial Intelligence Standards Committee.

His work focuses on bridging academic research and practical industry applications, leading innovative AI projects. As a Senior Member of IEEE, he actively participates in international conferences and workshops, contributing to global AI standards and best practices.

### Dr. Juan Carlos Martinez
**Research Professor · Universidad Tecnológica de Bolívar**

Electronic Engineer, Master in Electric Power from Universidad Industrial de Santander. Ph.D. from Northeastern University, Boston. Fulbright–DNP–Colciencias Scholar (2007). Researcher and professor at Universidad Tecnológica de Bolívar since 2004.

### Dr. Jairo Serrano Castañeda
**Research Professor · Universidad Tecnológica de Bolívar**

Systems Engineer from Universidad Tecnológica de Bolívar, Master's in Free Software from Universidad Autónoma de Bucaramanga. PhD candidate at Pontificia Universidad Javeriana, developing Social Simulations with AI in Multi-Agent Systems.

Over 23 years of professional experience. Senior Member of IEEE, member of the Colombian Computer Society, and co-founder of the IEEE Caribbean Colombia Section.
